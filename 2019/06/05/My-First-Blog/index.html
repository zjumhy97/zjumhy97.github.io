My First Blog

Posted on 2019-06-05 |

Some talk

Before I went to the university, coding was a remote thing to me. Computer just means browser and typewriter. I knew little about computer, Internet, algorithms…

When I learned the C language in ZJU, I feel really frustrated, I can’t finish my coding homework easily. Sometimes I wasted several hours to debug, finally I found that I type a bracket with chinese input method. It’s worse that I found the fact that my roommate started to learn coding when he was 12, he always skipped the C language class but he finished the homework really well. I lost my confidence.

I HATE CODING.

As a result, I didn’t get a good grade in my C language class. And when I chose my major in the end of my freshman year, I didn’t choose CS, I got into the Department of Control Science and Engineering. I believed that I will not meet any code in the rest of my life. How naive! In the fact, coding is the necessary skill in nowadays research for the most of STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) subjects. The most important thing is that, after a few years with MATLAB and Python, I found that coding is a really interesting thing. So I decide to open a blog to record my growing, not only coding, but also other things like CVX theory, Game Theory, etc.

Here we go！Let’s Rock！
